The hyper-local strategy of passing anti-abortion ordinances town by town is just another attempt by anti-abortion extremists to stigmatize and ban abortion in Texas, statewide. And while we’re a big state, these extreme laws may be coming to your city or town. It’s imperative that we collectively fight to keep abortion safe and legal at the federal, state, and local levels. We are doing our best to fight against these bans, but we need you to join us as well.
