Ready to Lead?

Consider joining our Abortion Access Academy and learn to be a leader in your community

The Texas Abortion Access Academy is a cohort-based, 8 week advocacy training program. The academy prepares community members to be leaders in the reproductive rights movement by helping them develop and internalize the skills they need to be powerful and effective advocates.

During the academy you will:

  • Learn from the pros. Professional advocates from Texas based reproductive rights organizations will share their knowledge and experience.
  • Get the tools you need to become a community leader
  • Join a beautiful community that values reproductive rights
  • Get certified as an abortion rights advocate
